# Accessibility Policy

## Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive digital experience that allows all users to access our content and services.

## Accessibility Standards

This website aims to conform to the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/) Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

## Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features on our website:

### Keyboard Navigation
- All functionality is available using a keyboard
- Logical tab order throughout the website
- Visible focus indicators on interactive elements
- Skip navigation links for easy content access

### Screen Reader Compatibility
- Semantic HTML markup for proper content structure
- Alternative text for images and graphics
- ARIA labels and landmarks where appropriate
- Clear and descriptive link text

### Visual Design
- Sufficient color contrast ratios
- Text resize capability up to 200%
- No reliance on color alone to convey information
- Clear and readable fonts
- Dark mode support to reduce eye strain

### Content Structure
- Logical heading hierarchy
- Clear and consistent navigation
- Descriptive page titles
- Meaningful error messages and form labels

### Media Content
- Captions for video content
- Transcripts for audio content
- Text alternatives for multimedia

## Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, there may be some limitations:

- Third-party embedded content may not be fully accessible
- Some legacy content may not meet current standards
- PDF documents may have varying accessibility levels

We are actively working to address these limitations and improve accessibility across all content.

## Assistive Technologies

This website has been tested with the following assistive technologies:

- Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
- Keyboard-only navigation
- Browser zoom functionality
- Voice recognition software

## Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly:

### Audit and Test
- Conduct regular accessibility audits
- Test with real users and assistive technologies
- Review new content for accessibility compliance

### Training and Awareness
- Provide accessibility training to our team
- Stay updated on accessibility best practices
- Incorporate accessibility in our design process

### Updates and Maintenance
- Address reported accessibility issues promptly
- Update content and features with accessibility in mind
- Monitor changes in accessibility standards

## Legal Compliance

We aim to comply with applicable accessibility laws and regulations, including:
- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
- Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
- European Accessibility Act
- Local and regional accessibility requirements

## Third-Party Content

While we strive to ensure accessibility throughout our website, some third-party content and services may not be fully under our control. We:
- Select accessible third-party services when possible
- Provide accessible alternatives where available
- Work with vendors to improve accessibility

## Mobile Accessibility

Our website is designed to be responsive and accessible on mobile devices, including:
- Touch-friendly interface elements
- Adequate spacing for touch targets
- Accessible mobile navigation
- Compatibility with mobile screen readers
